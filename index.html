About Me

Moving to Arizona and recreating myself in the tech field has been a life changing experience that is tapping into a part of myself that I have wanted to express but never knew how to. I feel as this is more of my chase side that displays itself when I work with anything technology based. Welcome to the portfolio for Chase that will display my ability to be a full stack developer.
